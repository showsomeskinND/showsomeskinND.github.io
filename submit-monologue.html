AUDITIONS FOR 2019 PERFORMANCE

Times and Location

Auditions for Show Some Skin: Drop the Wall will be held on the following dates:

Wednesday, November 28, 6:45-9:15 PM

Thursday, November 29, 6:00-8:30 PM

Saturday, December 1, 12:00-2:30 PM

Location: Andrews Auditorium (basement of Geddes Hall)

We are looking for everybody and anybody to audition. No prior acting experience is necessary. Four to five monologues will be provided to you. You will then have an opportunity to read over them and select one. The audition will be you reading the monologue in front of the production team. There is no preparation necessary.
